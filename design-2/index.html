Liquid Glass-ready Icon Composer

Transparent canvas, distinct layered groups (each color/depth as its own <g>), rounded caps/joins, no manual circular masks. Drop straight into Icon Composer and assign each layer a depth group; the system applies the glass material, specular highlights, and tint/dark/clear modes automatically.
